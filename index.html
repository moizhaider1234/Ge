II
chapter ii

Before Robinhood, we had the Grand Exchange — the first marketplace many of us ever learned to trade on.

Rows of stalls. Rows of prices, ticking up and down. A whole city built on the flip.

Buy low. Sell high. Flip rares. Watch the market breathe, long before anyone called it a "market."

Then the servers changed. New apps, new charts, new candles. The courtyard stayed in memory — but the chain never remembered it.

Until now.
